Building the future of dental revenue management

Who we are

Bridge Dental started with a simple belief: dental practices deserve better tools.

Our story

As practicing dentists, our founders saw firsthand how much time was lost to outdated RCM systems, manual verifications, and repetitive administrative work. They knew there had to be a smarter way — so they started building one.

Our mission

To give dental practices modern, practical revenue tools that simplify operations, reduce stress, and support sustainable growth — starting with the verification work that slows teams down every day.

What we're building

A modern revenue cycle for dental — starting with verification

Bridge Dental is a dentist-built platform focused on reducing the repetitive insurance work dental teams deal with every day. We're starting where it hurts most and expanding across the cycle from there.

The full revenue cycle we're building toward. Verification is live in early access today; the rest is on our roadmap.
